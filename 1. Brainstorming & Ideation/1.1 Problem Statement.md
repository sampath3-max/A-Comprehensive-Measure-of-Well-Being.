# Problem Statement

Economic indicators such as Gross Domestic Product (GDP) alone do not provide a complete picture of human well-being and development.

Human development is influenced by multiple factors, including health, education, and standard of living.

The Human Development Index (HDI) provides a broader measure of development by combining multiple dimensions of human development.

However, analyzing the relationship between socio-economic indicators and HDI can be difficult without a data-driven system.

Therefore, this project aims to develop a Machine Learning-based application that predicts the Human Development Index using selected socio-economic indicators.
